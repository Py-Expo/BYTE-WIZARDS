Groups

Science Group

The Science group focuses on mathematics, physics, chemistry, and biology. Students in this group often pursue careers in engineering, medicine, research, or related fields.

Commerce Group

The Commerce group emphasizes subjects like accounting, business studies, economics, and mathematics. Students in this group typically pursue careers in commerce, finance, accounting, or entrepreneurship.

Computer Science

The computer science group aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for careers in computer science, information technology, software development, and related fields. It provides a strong foundation for further studies in computer science at the university level or for entering the workforce in technology-related professions. Students who choose the computer science group in secondary education often have an interest in pursuing careers as software developers, system analysts, network administrators, or other roles in the IT industry.

Accountance Group

The Accountancy group is designed to prepare students for careers in accounting, finance, auditing, and related fields. It provides a solid foundation for further studies in accounting at the university level or for entering the workforce in roles such as accountants, auditors, financial analysts, or finance managers. Students who choose the Accountancy group often have an interest in working with financial data, analyzing economic trends, and contributing to the financial management of organizations.
